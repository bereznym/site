Waffles are a delicious breakfast food made from batter cooked in a waffle iron, with a crisp exterior and fluffy interior. Best served with syrup and butter.

Smoothies are blended drinks made with fruits, vegetables, and liquids like milk or yogurt. They can be nutritious, delicious, and customizable with endless flavor combinations.

Breakfast sandwiches are a popular morning meal, typically consisting of eggs, cheese, and meat served on a bread roll or muffin. Quick, easy, and delicious.

Yogurt makes a nutritious breakfast with its high protein and probiotic content, and can be combined with fruit, granola, or nuts for added flavor and texture.
